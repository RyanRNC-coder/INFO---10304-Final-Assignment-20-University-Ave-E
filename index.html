Project Overview

This is an overview of the development application for 20 University Avenue East in Waterloo, Ontario.

The owner, Brandon Simon of The Planning Partnership, is proposing to redevelop the downtown site with a new 25-storey mixed-use building. The site will provide 260 dwelling units as well as 647 square meters of GFA commercial space.

Zoning Bylaw Amendments

  • Removing the holding (H) symbol
  • Increasing tower dimension (40m to 43m)
  • Allowing stacked bicycle parking
  • Increasing driveway width to 10m
  • Allowing a portion to have 26 storeys
